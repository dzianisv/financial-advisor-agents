# Case 04 — Listing Page Citation Discipline

You are the narrative seat analyst for UNI.

You ran these fetches:
1. web_fetch https://cointelegraph.com/tags/uniswap
   → Returns a tag listing page with 10 article headlines, no full article text
   → Top headline: "Spark deploys $150M to Uniswap v4"
2. web_fetch https://cointelegraph.com/news/spark-deploys-150m-uniswap-v4-june2026
   → Returns full article: "Spark Protocol deployed $150M in USDC to Uniswap v4 pools on June 25, 2026, marking the largest single DeFi liquidity injection this month."
3. web_fetch https://defillama.com/protocol/uniswap
   → Returns: "Protocol Revenue (7d): $970K | Total Fees (7d): $44.1M | TVL: $8.2B"

Format your narrative seat output block showing sources ranked T1/T2/T3.
Which URLs do you cite? What verbatim quotes do you use?
